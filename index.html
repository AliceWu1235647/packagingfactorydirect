Expert FAQ

Professional answers to common custom packaging questions.

What is GS1 Serialization?

It's a system where every single box receives a unique, trackable QR or Data Matrix code, ensuring anti-counterfeit compliance and supply chain transparency.

How do collapsible boxes save costs?

They ship flat, reducing volume by up to 75% compared to traditional rigid boxes, which significantly lowers sea and air freight costs.

What is the MOQ for custom orders?

We support growing brands with a flexible Low MOQ of just 500 pieces for fully customized packaging solutions.

Are your materials eco-friendly?
