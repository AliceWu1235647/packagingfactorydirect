Custom Printed Bakery & Pastry Packaging

Our custom bakery packaging solutions are engineered to showcase your artisanal creations while ensuring they remain fresh and protected. We specialize in high-quality paperboard boxes and matching carrier sets that provide a cohesive and professional brand identity for your bakery, cafe, or pâtisserie.

B2B Features & Customization:

  • Food-Grade Material: Thickened white cardboard (300-450gsm) with odorless and safe coating.
  • Bespoke Printing: HD offset printing for intricate designs, logos, and colorful patterns using eco-friendly inks.
  • Versatile Styles: Auto-lock bottom boxes, folding cartons, handle boxes, and matching paper carry bags.
  • Protective Coating: Optional grease-proof and moisture-resistant internal barriers for buttery pastries.
  • Window Options: Clear PET window patches available to showcase your products inside.
  • MOQ: 500 units for digital/small batch; wholesale pricing for bulk orders.
