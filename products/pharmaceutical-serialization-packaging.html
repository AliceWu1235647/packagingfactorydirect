Advanced Pharmaceutical Packaging Solutions

Secure Every Product. Track Every Package. One Box, One Unique Code.

We manufacture custom pharmaceutical folding cartons and medical packaging boxes with advanced serialization and variable data printing (VDP) capabilities. Our technology helps pharmaceutical, healthcare, and nutraceutical brands achieve compliance, product security, and supply chain transparency.

Smart Manufacturing Capabilities:

  • GS1-Compliant Barcodes: Accurate printing of linear barcodes and 2D Data Matrix codes.
  • Serialization: Unique serial number and QR code on every single box for 'One Box One Code' traceability.
  • Anti-Counterfeit Features: Holographic foils, tamper-evident seals, and consumer verification systems.
  • VDP Technology: High-speed variable data printing for batch numbers, expiry dates, and unique IDs.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Supporting DSCSA and international tracking standards.

Industry Applications:

  • Prescription & OTC Drugs: Secure cartons for pharmaceutical manufacturers.
  • Medical Aesthetics: Specialized packaging for Hyaluronic Acid, Dermal Fillers, and Botulinum Toxin.
  • Healthcare & Nutraceuticals: Premium boxes for supplements and medical devices.
  • Injectable Products: Durable and protective packaging for clinical supplies.
