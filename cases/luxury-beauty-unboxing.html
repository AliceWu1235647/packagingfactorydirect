75% Shipping Savings for Luxury Beauty Brands

Optimizing the Global Supply Chain with Collapsible Rigid Packaging.

The Client

A fast-growing luxury skincare brand based in New York needed a high-end rigid box but was struggling with the astronomical costs of air-shipping pre-assembled boxes from China.

The Innovation

We designed a collapsible magnetic rigid box using 1200gsm premium greyboard. The box ships 100% flat and features hidden high-strength magnets and 3M adhesive corners that allow for assembly in less than 5 seconds.

Cost Impact:

Before: 1 container = 10,000 pre-assembled boxes.
After: 1 container = 40,000 collapsible boxes.

The Results

The client reduced their total landing cost by 62% while receiving glowing reviews for the premium weighted feel and magnetic "click" of the box. This case proves that sustainability and luxury can coexist with logistical efficiency.
