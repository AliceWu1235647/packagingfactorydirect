Reducing Counterfeit Risk by 40% with GS1 Serialization

A Case Study in Intelligent Pharmaceutical Packaging Compliance.

The Challenge

A leading pharmaceutical client in Germany was facing significant losses due to counterfeit products entering the Asian market. Their existing packaging lacked unique tracking identifiers, making it impossible to verify authentic units at the consumer level.

The Solution

Packaging Factory Direct implemented a full-scale GS1 Data Matrix serialization system. Using our HP Indigo digital press and high-speed VDP (Variable Data Printing) workflow, we produced over 1 million folding cartons, each featuring a unique, encrypted QR code and serial number.

  • GTIN Integration: Fully compliant with international GS1 standards.
  • Anti-Counterfeit: Tamper-evident holographic seals were added to both ends of the carton.
  • Cloud Verification: Linked each code to a secure database for instant consumer scanning.

The Results

Within 6 months of implementation, the brand reported a 40% drop in suspected counterfeit reports and achieved 100% compliance with new regional track-and-trace regulations.
