Inside Our High-Tech Facility

Experience the precision and scale of a world-class packaging factory. From design to delivery, we maintain a 100,000-grade cleanroom environment for food and medical products.

Heidelberg Offset Excellence

Our production floor is anchored by the latest Heidelberg XL 7-color offset presses. These machines deliver unmatched color consistency and speed, capable of producing over 50,000 units per hour for high-volume B2B orders.

  • Precision ink control systems
  • Automated plate changing
  • Sustainable soy-based ink compatibility

Digital VDP & HP Indigo

For serialization and short-run luxury orders, we utilize HP Indigo technology. This allows for high-resolution digital printing with variable data (VDP), ensuring every box in a series can be unique without expensive plate costs.

  • Serialized QR & Data Matrix codes
  • Micro-text anti-counterfeit features
  • Wide color gamut for brand accuracy
